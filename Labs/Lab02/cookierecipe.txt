Broiled_chocolate_chip_cookies

#Materials
baking sheet
parchment paper
2 sticks salted butter
2 cups sugar
1 tbsp molasses
2 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla extract
2 tsp salted1 tsp baking soda
2.5 cups bread flour
12 oz bag chocolate chips

#Methods
1. preheat over to 375 F
2. melt butter and mix in the other ingredients until a soft dough is formed. refrigerate the dough for 30 minutes or more
3. roll the dough into gold-ball sized balls and flatten the tops with your hands, place on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper
4. turn the broiler on its high setting, put the cookies int he oven and broil until the tops are golden, remove from oven and allow to cool
5. set oven to bake, put cookies back in the oven for 10-12 minutes, remove and cool
